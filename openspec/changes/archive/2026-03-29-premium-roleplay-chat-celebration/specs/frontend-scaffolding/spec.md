# frontend-scaffolding (Delta Spec)

## MODIFIED

- **REQ-PREMIUM-CONVERSATION-VISUAL-LANGUAGE**: Conversation and completion surfaces in the practice experience SHALL use a premium tutor/coach visual language with bright, clean panels, restrained accents, strong typography, and minimal decorative noise.
- **REQ-MOTION-RESTRAINT-IN-PREMIUM-UX**: Streaming text and celebration motion SHALL feel polished and intentional rather than playful or chaotic.
- **REQ-REDUCED-MOTION-SUPPORT**: Frontend motion-heavy practice states SHOULD provide simplified behavior when the user prefers reduced motion.

### Scenario: Roleplay looks like premium tutoring chat
- **WHEN** the learner enters the AI roleplay surface
- **THEN** the visual treatment feels like a premium tutoring conversation rather than a generic chat app or gamified exercise

### Scenario: Celebration remains polished
- **WHEN** completion fireworks are shown
- **THEN** the motion supports a premium sense of achievement
- **AND** the interface does not become visually noisy or distracting
