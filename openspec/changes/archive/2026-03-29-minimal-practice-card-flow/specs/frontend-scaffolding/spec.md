# frontend-scaffolding (Delta Spec)

## MODIFIED

- **REQ-LEARNING-VISUAL-SIMPLIFICATION**: Learning-shell surfaces SHALL avoid heavy glow, oversized gradients, and overly transparent panels. The visual system SHALL prefer calmer solid surfaces, subtle borders, and restrained accent usage.
- **REQ-LEARNING-SURFACE-READABILITY**: Accent color usage SHALL emphasize actions and status without overpowering page readability in either light or dark mode.
