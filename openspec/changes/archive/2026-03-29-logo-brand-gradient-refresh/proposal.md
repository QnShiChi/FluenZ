## Why

The current orange-led branding feels too bright and visually harsh for the product direction. The team now has an official `fluenz-logo.png` asset and a clearer brand palette derived from that logo:
- turquoise `#0CCFC3`
- cyan-blue `#0AA4D0`
- indigo `#6050E0`
- violet `#8020F0`

The UI should align with the official brand colors and use the new logo in key branding locations.

## What Changes

- Replace the current orange-dominant brand palette with a logo-aligned gradient palette spanning turquoise, cyan-blue, indigo, and violet.
- Update semantic brand tokens so core UI accents follow the new identity while remaining centrally configurable.
- Adopt `fluenz-logo.png` as the official product logo in the app header branding area.
- Replace the current favicon with the official `fluenz-logo.png` asset or a favicon derived from it.

## Capabilities

### Modified Capabilities
- `frontend-scaffolding`: update centralized brand tokens and official logo asset usage

## Impact

- **Frontend**: theme tokens, header branding, favicon metadata, public branding assets
- **Branding**: product visuals become aligned with the official FluenZ identity
