# practice-session (Delta Spec)

## MODIFIED

- **REQ-PRACTICE-COMPLETION-FIREWORKS-CONTRAST**: The completion fireworks SHALL use a warmer, higher-contrast celebratory palette that remains clearly visible against the application's dominant cool-toned interface.
- **REQ-PRACTICE-COMPLETION-FIREWORKS-VISIBILITY**: The completion fireworks configuration SHALL be strong enough to be noticed immediately on screen entry while still preserving readability of the completion summary.

### Scenario: Fireworks are visually noticeable
- **WHEN** the learner enters the completion screen
- **THEN** the fireworks are clearly visible without requiring close inspection
- **AND** they do not visually disappear into the surrounding blue/teal palette

### Scenario: Fireworks remain readable behind content
- **WHEN** the celebration runs
- **THEN** the learner can still read the score ring, summary metrics, and review list without obstruction
